GENERAL TIPS

Follow Balanced diet plan

Follow a frequent small meal diet pattern. Eat at an interval of two hours so that your blood sugar levels don't drop to down. Consume healthily home cooked food.

Engage in Active Lifestyle

Doing more physical activities can help in controlling diabetes, but greater benefit comes from a fitness program.

Do regular exercise

include at least 30-45 minutes workout in everyday be it yoga, brisk walk, or any other activity.

Drink plenty of water

Drink plenty of water and other drinks like lemon water, green tea, herbal tea and keep yourself hydrated.

Avoid Starch

Use no starch or low starch vegetables e.g carrots, beans, gourds, green chillies, capsicums, tomatoes, radish, asparagus, cauliflower, brocolli etc. and strictly avoid potatoes,yam, colocasia or other starchy items (food items with low glycaemic index).

Use low salty Food

Avoid excess salt and salty products. High level of salt leads to high blood pressure problems. And include garlic, black pepper,powder, roasted cumin seed powder mint, lemon juice to enhance the taste.

Things to avoid by Diabetic Patient

Tobacco, Smoking, Avoid canned food, Packaged food items, malted beverages, cold drinks, fruit juice drinks, avoid excess salt, Avoid alcohol.
