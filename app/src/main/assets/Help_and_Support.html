Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Does the app supports synchronization of data?

Yes it supports it. Data synchronization means making data available on different platforms, different devices etc and making sure that all are having same data and there is no data loss. If you want to synchonize data, go to settings on home page, click on data and sync, then you can click on sync frequency of your choice or you can configure using system sync settings.

 2. How do i join diabetes forum in this application?

Go to settings on home page, then tap on the overflow (3 vertical dots) icon in the top right corner of the screen, then click on forum, oncle clicked it will take you to a website, where you can sign up and join.

3. What units of measure am i supposed to enter on medicine page?

Units of measure includes capsules, tablets,gram and mg.

4. What values am i supposed to nter in blood sugar page on field named measured?

You are supposed to enter when you took the blood sugar test, this includes:- before breakfast, after breakfast, before lunch, after lunch, before dinner and after dinner.
